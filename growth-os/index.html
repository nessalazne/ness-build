FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions.

Do I need to know how to code?

No. GrowthOS is delivered as a complete platform. During Week 1, Ness guides you through installation and deployment. Weeks 2–4 focus on customization, troubleshooting, and implementation. As your confidence grows, Claude Code makes customizing the platform much easier than building everything from scratch.

Do I need a Mac?

Yes. GrowthOS is currently developed and officially supported on macOS. All live implementation sessions are demonstrated on macOS. Windows support is planned but has not yet been officially tested.

What do I receive immediately after I purchase?

You'll receive access to GrowthOS, your license key, the complete source code, setup instructions, bonus courses, and recordings. During the next live implementation week, Ness will help you get everything installed, configured, and running.

What exactly do I own?

You receive the complete GrowthOS source code, every file, every workflow, your license, and lifetime access to the training and recordings included with your purchase.

Can I customize the platform?

Yes. That's one of the biggest advantages of GrowthOS. You can modify the interface, prompts, workflows, integrations, and AI systems to fit your own business using Claude Code.

Are the future AI systems included?

Yes. As a Founding Member, you'll receive the additional AI systems in the GrowthOS roadmap as they're completed at no additional cost.

Is the Builders Club required?

No. Builders Club is completely optional. Your Founding Member purchase already includes the platform, roadmap updates, recordings, and implementation program. Builders Club is simply for ongoing live support and continued development sessions after your first month.

What does it cost to run GrowthOS?

Hosting starts around $5/month on Railway. AI services such as OpenRouter, ElevenLabs, Gemini, or HeyGen are billed separately based on your own usage—just like they would be if you used those services independently.

Can I use GrowthOS for client work?

The Founding Member (GrowthOS Standard) license is intended for your own business. Agency and White Label licenses are planned for customers who want broader commercial deployment rights.

How many computers can I install GrowthOS on?

The GrowthOS Standard license includes one licensed development computer and one production deployment. You can create multiple GrowthOS projects on your licensed development computer, but the license does not permit installing the software on multiple computers or sharing the source code with others.

Can you build and configure GrowthOS for me?

Yes. If you'd rather skip the implementation, you can choose our optional Done-For-You Setup service. We'll install GrowthOS, configure your environment, connect your APIs, deploy your platform, and have everything ready for you to use. You'll still receive the complete source code, your license, and full ownership of your GrowthOS platform. Done-For-You Setup is available separately →

If you're unsure whether GrowthOS is right for you, please email us at ness@digicuratoragency.com before purchasing. We're happy to answer your questions so you can buy with confidence. Because GrowthOS includes immediately accessible digital software, source code, and downloadable assets, purchases are non-refundable.
